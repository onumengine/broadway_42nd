Who We Are ...

It started as one young undergrad taking pictures of everything - coke bottles, wrist bands, insects, whatever you can think, to taking unusually creative shots of his friends, to taking shots of clothes for online sops to eventually capturing moments and feelings all around the city. After capturing the night energy at Rumuokwuta, that grad student went on to find more people that shared his passion of capturing feeling and energy, forever engraving them in digital pictures.



Made with love in PHC

Instead of a company, we like to call ourselves a group of individuals with diverse talents who share a passion for moments. We realize that in our fast-paced world, moments have become even faster fleeting and we love to capture those important moments in our lives, society and nature and preserve those feelings in the form of pictures. Painting moments is our art, photography is our brush. We are a group of like minds doing something we love together.
